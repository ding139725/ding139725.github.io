Type

Basketball is a hand-centered physical confrontation sport that is the core sport of the Olympic Games. It was invented by James Naismith, a physical education teacher at the YMCA Training School in Springfield, Massachusetts, on December 21, 1891.

History

In 1896, basketball spread to Tianjin, China.

In 1904, the first basketball exhibition was held at the St. Louis Olympics.

In 1936, basketball was listed as an official event in the Berlin Olympic Games, and China sent a basketball team to participate in the Olympic Games for the first time

Field stroke

The standard basketball court should be 28m×15m rectangular court each line width is 5cm.

The ground should be solid and flat, with no obstacles within at least 2m of the surrounding area.

Length: 28m

Width: 15m

Wire frame width: 5cm

Middle circle radius: 1.8m

Free throw line: From the inside edge of the end line to its very edge

5.80m on the side and 3.60m in length

Basket: the diameter of the inner edge is at least 0.45m, and the number is at most 0.457m

Basketball hoop height: 3.05m
